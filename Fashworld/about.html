Who We Are

Fashworld is a platform that aims to give start-up "streetwear" fashion brands an opportunity to make their business profitable by offering sevices thtat will improve their internet presence.

About Us

FASHWORLD The name is a combination of the words fashion and world. This is a trendy business name, it speaks to the aim of the business, which is to create a platform (world) for fashion and clothing brands to advertise and sell their merchandise. FASHWORLD is an online clothing store that serves as an intermediary for small clothing brands to sell and advertise their merchandise. The platform is a B2B (Business to Business) and B2C (Business to Customer) service. FASHWORLD, as stated above, will provide clothing branding with advertising, promotion and selling merchandise for subscribed businesses to their customers for a fee. Current systems are only targeted towards established and popular brands. Other systems only create a platform for their own products. FASHWORLD differs to its alternatives in the sense that it is targeted towards lesser known brands to establish themselves and improve their profitability in a very competitive market.

Mission

The mission of the company is to provide a market for local brands and a platform for consumers to discover quality and trendy brands.
